Peer-to-peer equipment rental

Borrow the gear. Skip the price tag.

GearShare connects people who own cameras, tools, and outdoor equipment with neighbors who need them for a weekend, a shoot, or a single job. List what you're not using. Rent what you need.

List your gear

Owners post equipment with a daily rate, a location, and a short description. Toggle availability whenever it's busy.

Book with dates

Renters pick a start and end date. GearShare checks for overlapping bookings automatically before confirming.

Review after pickup

Once a rental is marked complete, the renter can leave a rating and a short note for the next person.

How a rental moves through the system

A booking request is pending until the owner makes a decision. The owner can approve or reject it. Once the rental period has passed, the owner marks it completed, which unlocks reviews for the renter.
